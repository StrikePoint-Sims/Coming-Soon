From the owner

I'm Mike. Two young kids, a full schedule, and a New England winter that takes another six months off the calendar. Getting to a course is hard. Getting better is harder.

A few years back I built a launch monitor setup over my garage so I could keep playing after the kids bedtime. It's made me a better golfer and definitely piqued a bit of interest.

Trouble is, the bay shares space with my office. People who hear about it want to come try, and it doesn't work out. But I understand the demand and the closest sims in eastern Connecticut are a ways away. I'm making StrikePoint Sims to be a nearby luxurious golf haven for anyone. Private bays as the default so you feel like you're with your buddies at your home away from home.

We're opening Fall 2026 in Colchester. To our neighbors in Lebanon, Hebron, Marlborough, East Hampton, Glastonbury, Salem, Bozrah, and anyone else tired of the long drive or the winter layoff: We hope to see you soon.

Common questions

Everything you need to know.

When do you open?

Fall 2026. Founding members get first access to opening week slots.

What's Trackman iO?

Ceiling-mounted dual-radar launch monitor. It's the most accurate golf simulation technology commercially available, with over 30 data points per swing on the same system used on the PGA Tour. Ball flight, spin, club path, everything. Full breakdown →

How does 24/7 access work?

Book from an app, get a unique door code, show up, play, leave. Streamlined tech with no hassles.

What does it cost?

Walk-in rates will be $45-60 depending on time. Off-peak hours are weekdays before 5pm and nights. StrikePoint OG members lock in below-launch rates for 24 months, with a 14-day money-back guarantee.

What is a StrikePoint OG?

A Founding Member of StrikePoint Sims. Founding members lock in below-launch rates for 24 months, with a money-back guarantee.

Do I have to pay to join the waitlist?

No. We require a card to reserve a spot, but monthly billing for Founding members doesn't begin until we open, and the first 14 days come with a money-back guarantee if we aren't for you.

Can I bring guests?

Yes. Every membership includes guest privileges. Full guest policy publishes at launch.

Where are you opening?

We are planning to open in Colchester, CT.
