Key Observations — Through Year End

Cash-flow positive by November. October is the only operating month with negative NOI ($2,700 deficit). By November, revenue exceeds operating expenses and the business is self-sustaining on an operating basis.

Cumulative position reaches ~$0 by December. Owner invests ~$27K peak in June, recovers when SBA funds in July, then Oct operating loss trims reserves before Nov–Dec surplus returns to roughly break-even.

Trackman repayment is the critical variable. $70,485 due ~Feb 2027. SBA reserve covers ~$47K by December; the remaining ~$23K needs Jan–Feb operating cash or personal funds. Peak season should make this achievable.
