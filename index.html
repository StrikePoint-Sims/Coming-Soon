StrikePoint is what I wanted to exist: three private bays, tour-grade tech, and the ability to book a 6am session on a Tuesday without asking permission from anyone.

Questions

When do you open?

November 2026. Founders get first access to opening week slots.

What's Trackman iO?

Ceiling-mounted dual-radar launch monitor. It's the most accurate golf simulation technology commercially available — over 30 data points per swing, the same system used on the PGA Tour. Ball flight, spin, club path, everything.

How does 24/7 access work?

Book from an app, get a unique door code, show up, play, leave. Cameras on common areas. No cameras in the bays.

What does it cost?

Walk-in rates and membership pricing publish closer to opening. Founders lock in below-launch rates for 24 months.

Do I have to pay to join the waitlist?

No. No card required. Monthly billing for Founders doesn't begin until we open.

Can I bring guests?

Yes. Every membership includes guest privileges. Full guest policy publishes at launch.

What about lessons?

Planning to partner with local PGA teaching pros for optional lessons in the bays. Members will get first access.
