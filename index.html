From the owner

I'm Mike. I love golf. I'm not great at it, but I've loved it for over a decade. However, with two young kids and a New England winter I didn't get to play much. So I built myself a golf sim.

My home sim is a little rough around the edges, but I could play after the kids bedtime. It's made me a better golfer and is definitely of interest among friends.

Now I'm building StrikePoint Sims as the sim I want to exist. Every sim place nearby kinda feels like a bar or a driving range.

This is different. You book a time, walk in, and the bay is yours. No one standing behind you, no front desk, no waiting around, no being upsold expensive drinks. Just a clean setup where you can practice or play whenver fits your schedule.

I'm building it to be your own home sim away from home. Something you and your buddies can enjoy.

Common questions

Everything you need to know.

When do you open?

Fall 2026. Founding members get first access to opening week slots.

What's Trackman iO?

Ceiling-mounted dual-radar launch monitor. It's the most accurate golf simulation technology commercially available, with over 30 data points per swing on the same system used on the PGA Tour. Ball flight, spin, club path, everything. Full breakdown →

How does 24/7 access work?

Golfers reserve a bay online, enter with a secure access code, and use their scheduled bay independently. Common areas are monitored for security, and support will be available if something needs attention. The experience is built to be private, efficient, and self-directed.

What does it cost?

Walk-in rates will be $45-60 depending on time. Off-peak is weekdays before 5pm and every night between 10pm and 6am. Membership runs from Range (off-peak access) through Standard and Elite. Founding Members lock in a permanent discount off our standard monthly pricing, with a 14-day money-back guarantee.

Who are the Founding 20?

The first twenty members of StrikePoint Sims. Founding Members lock in a permanent monthly discount off our standard rates — for the life of their membership, as long as it stays active — and get permanent recognition on the wall at the facility. Once the twenty spots are filled, Founding pricing closes for good.

Is this a waitlist or am I committing?

It's a real reservation. Your card is saved at signup but not charged until we open. Once we open, your first month bills automatically and you have 14 days to cancel for a full refund. We do this so we know who's actually in — we'd rather have 20 committed Founding Members than 200 names on a list.

Can I bring guests?

Yes. Every membership includes guest privileges. Full guest policy publishes at launch.

Where are you opening?

We are working toward opening in Colchester, CT.
