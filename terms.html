6. Facility Rules and Code of Conduct

The following rules apply to all Users and guests at all times. Violation of any rule may result in immediate termination of the session without refund and suspension or cancellation of membership.

6a. Capacity and Supervision

  • Maximum occupancy is five persons per bay.
  • Only one person may be in the hitting area (in front of the impact screen) at any time. All other persons must remain behind the hitting mat.
  • Users under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times.

6b. Safety

  • Always check your surroundings before swinging. Confirm no person or object is within the arc of your swing, including backswing and follow-through.
  • All shots must be directed toward the impact screen. Hitting golf balls at any other surface is prohibited.
  • Practice swings must only occur within the designated hitting area.
  • No throwing of golf balls or other objects.
  • Do not swing clubs in corridors, common areas, or any location outside the simulator bay.

6c. Equipment and Facility

  • Treat all simulator equipment with care. Do not strike, throw, or deliberately damage any simulator component, screen, mat, furniture, or hardware.
  • Clean shoes and clubs are required. No outdoor shoes in hitting areas during inclement weather months.
  • Return all provided equipment (balls, tees) at the end of your session.
  • No food or beverages inside the hitting area or near simulator hardware.

6d. Access

  • Access credentials issued to members are personal and non-transferable. Sharing credentials with any non-member is a material breach of this Agreement.
  • Members may bring guests subject to the guest policy in Section 7.
  • Do not prop open or tamper with access-controlled doors.

6e. Conduct

  • The Facility is monitored by security cameras at all times. Recording is ongoing.
  • Disruptive, threatening, or illegal conduct will result in immediate access termination and, where appropriate, notification to law enforcement.
  • No smoking, vaping, or use of controlled substances anywhere in the Facility.
